Triangle Homes Account Deletion Request

Users of Triangle Homes can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to support@trianglehomes.in
  2. Use subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include registered email or phone number

Data deleted

  • User profile information
  • Authentication data
  • Saved preferences
  • Uploaded personal documents/images

Data retained temporarily

The following may be retained for up to 90 days for legal, fraud prevention, finance, or audit purposes:

  • Booking history
  • Transaction records
  • Compliance logs

Deletion requests are processed within 7–30 business days after verification.
